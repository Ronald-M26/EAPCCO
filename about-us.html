MR APOLLO AFRICA

Africa Apollo is the current Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of EAPCCO. He was appointed in January 2024 to double as Head of INTERPOL Regional Bureau Nairobi as well as CEO EAPCCO for a term of three years. He is a seconded Senior Police Officer from the Rwanda National Police. He has an experience of more than 26 years in Policing at home and internationally.
