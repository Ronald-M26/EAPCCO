ABOUT US

The Eastern Africa Police Chiefs Cooperation Organization (EAPCCO) is a regional police body whose membership consists of Chiefs of
Police of the 14 countries. It was established in 1998 with the aim of harmonizing, strengthening police co-operation and joint strategies, sharing of crime related
information and harmonization of laws in order to enhance the capacity of Law Enforcement Agencies to combat transnational organized crime.
